## Writing Principle

Moi case study nen co 2 lop noi dung:

| Lop noi dung | Muc dich |
| --- | --- |
| Technical troubleshooting | Cho thay biet dung command, doc trieu chung, khoanh vung va fix loi. |
| Real operations impact | Cho thay minh nghi den downtime, SLA, change management va quy trinh van hanh. |
